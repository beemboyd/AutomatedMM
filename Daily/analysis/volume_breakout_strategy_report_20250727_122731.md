## Strategy Description
- **Entry:** Take position when hourly volume breaks out (>1.5x average volume)
- **Position Sizing:** Double position each time price crosses previous hourly close
- **Exit:** Close all positions when price drops below previous candle low

## Risk Assessment
### Pros:
- Capitalizes on momentum when volume confirms price movement
- Clear entry and exit rules
- Potential for large gains in trending markets

### Cons:
- **High Risk:** Position doubling can lead to exponential losses
- **Capital Intensive:** Requires significant capital as positions grow
- **Whipsaw Risk:** False breakouts can trigger entries that reverse quickly
- **Slippage:** Large positions may face liquidity issues
